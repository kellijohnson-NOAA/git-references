# Summary
Uses [@essington_von_2001](notes/notes_processed/Essington2001.md) methods to estimate consumption rates for tuna, while incorporating Hg-at-size, to test the hypothesis that key parameters governing growth and Hg dynamics may be estimable from patterns of Hg-at-size data. 

# Methods 
Bioenergetics models produce an estimated consumption rate-at-size, while contaminant models relate changes in Hg-at-size to consumption rate and growth.
