- statistical age structured multi-species population dynamics model

- provides excellent reviews on multi-species stock assessment methods and a historical time line of diet analysis

- uses maximum likelihood methods to estimate parameters regarding catch, stomach contents, and abundance in a single model allowing for error in all measurements

- Predation: pollock is a major prey of cod and flounder

- simplifications:
    - time-invariant length-at-age
    - time-invariant weight-at-age
    - time-invariant selectivity
    - survey catchability was set to 1 for all species
    - predator ingestion rate (@aydin_comparison_2007 @essington_von_2001) must be constant because length- and weight-at-age are time-invariant
