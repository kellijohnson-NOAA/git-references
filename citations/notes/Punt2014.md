# Summary

Control rules require that $B_{MSY}$ and $F_{MSY}$ can be estimated, which is extremely difficult
for invertebrate stocks because stock-recruitment relationships are often poorly defined or very uncertain.
Stock assessments were performed for five Alaskan crab stocks and subsequently three methods were applied to 
estimate $B_{MSY}$ and $F_{MSY}$: (a) dynamic $B_0$, (b) stock-recruit, and 
(c) stock-recruit with environmental relationships.
