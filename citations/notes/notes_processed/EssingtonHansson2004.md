Results
=======

Functional responses integrating local interactions over the entire population may have a different shape than the functional responses occurring at the scales of local interactions. Age specific prey rates were different for each prey, with older cod preying on young herring and young cod preying on young sprat. Did not find satiation and models that included satiation led to a poorer fit, which is inline with other studies showing piscivores have low consumption rates (Essington et al. (2000)) and can exceed their maximum rates when feeding on patchy prey.

Essington, T. E., Hodgson, J. R., and Kitchell, J. F. 2000. Role of satiation in the functional response of a piscivore, largemouth bass (*Micropterus salmoides*). Canadian Journal of Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences, 57: 548–556. <http://www.nrcresearchpress.com/doi/abs/10.1139/f99-289> (Accessed 11 October 2013).
